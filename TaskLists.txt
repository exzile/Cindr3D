## Storage-only items (documented reason, not TODO)

- **minimumSpeed** — floor for cool throttling; we don't throttle against
  minLayerTime, so the floor has nothing to floor against.
- **noSkinInZGaps** — our layer-count skin detector inherently doesn't
  produce skin in z-gaps.
- **supportJoinDistance** — support merges all overhang tris into one
  bbox (equivalent to infinite join distance).
- **zSeamOnVertex** — our seam picker always returns vertex indices;
  `true` is the inherent behavior.
